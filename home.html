About me

I received the B.Eng. degree from South China University of Technology in 2013, the M.Eng. degree from Sun Yat-sen University in 2016, and the Ph.D. degree in electrical and electronic engineering from The University of Hong Kong in 2021. I was visiting research student with the City University of Hong Kong in 2014 and Aalborg University in 2016.

Currently, I am a Research Fellow with the School of Electrical and Electronic Engineering, Nanyang Technological University. I have extensive experience in power electronics and wireless power. I have authored/co-authored more than 30 technical papers, including 11 first-authored papers in top-tier journals of power electronics. I serve as a peer reviewer for various IEEE transactions and journals.

I won the Ph.D. Thesis Talk Award from IEEE Power Electronics Society in 2022, the The University of Hong Kong Power Engineering Prize in 2020, and China National Scholarship in 2016.

Research interests

  • Power electronics

  • Wireless power transfer

  • Renewable energy
